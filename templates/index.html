📚 Dataset Overview

• Source: UCI Student Performance (Math)
• Records: 395 students
• Features used: sex, age, studytime, failures, absences, G1, G2, goout, Medu, Fedu
• Target: pass/fail (G3 ≥ 10 → pass)

📝 How to Predict

  1. Fill in each field with the student’s data.
  2. Ranges are shown in placeholders and descriptions below each input.
  3. Click “Predict” to see whether the student is likely to pass.
